Led by veterans of the managed hosting business, we have taken our 20+ years of experience in complex hosting environments and made it our mission to simplify the cloud.

Our Mission

Cloud, founded in 2014, is on a mission to empower developers and businesses by simplifying the deployment of infrastructure via its advanced cloud platform. Cloud is strategically located in 185 datacenters around the globe and provides frictionless provisioning of public cloud, storage and single-tenant bare metal.

Cloud has made it our priority to offer a standardized highly reliable high performance cloud compute environment in all of the cities we serve. Launching a cloud server environment in 185 cities around the globe has never been easier!
